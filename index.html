About Technimble

Technimble at NSRIT College is an exciting and dynamic technical event designed to challenge and showcase students' skills in coding, engineering, robotics, and data science. Participants take part in a series of engaging challenges, including coding competitions, hardware tasks, and knowledge-based quizzes, all crafted to test their technical abilities and foster creativity. The event promotes teamwork, collaboration, and problem-solving, providing a unique platform for students to gain hands-on experience, refine their skills, and interact with industry professionals. Technimble offers a stimulating and competitive environment that not only enhances participants' technical expertise but also prepares them for future careers in the rapidly evolving technology landscape.

Venue

NSRIT, Sontyam, Visakhapatnam.
